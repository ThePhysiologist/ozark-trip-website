About Ozark Folk Center

The Ozark Folk Center State Park is dedicated to preserving and presenting traditional Ozark music, crafts, and culture. Located in Mountain View (the "Folk Music Capital of the World"), visitors can:

  • Watch 20+ artisans demonstrate traditional crafts
  • Hear live folk music on the Blacksmith Stage throughout the day
  • Attend concerts at the 1,000-seat Ozark Highlands Theater
  • Explore the Heritage Herb Garden
  • Dine at the Skillet Restaurant for Southern cooking

Hours (Aug): Craft Village: Tue-Sat, 10am-5pm. Live music throughout the day.
